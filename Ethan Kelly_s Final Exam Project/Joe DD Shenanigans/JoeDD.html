Second o' all, D&D isa' roleplay game. Joe ere' is a penguin.

Now that's done, let me exit that weird dwarf persona. Joe has been playing D&D for a while, and he has done alot. He has fought baddies, tamed a demon dog, and killed an innkeeper *in a dream*. That didn't happen in reality thank goodness, because it would have ruined the story and the entire game. He has collected quite a lot of stuff, but the adventure is coming to an end, since Mr. Renfrow's class is coming to an end. Joe is usually in a predicament, since he can't speak to his friend Christopher directly. This means he needs a translator, or hand signals. The bad thing is, he doesn't have opposable thumbs... In the end, poor Joe has no end to his issues.

Poor Joe.
